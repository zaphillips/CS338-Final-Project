Mario Party Superstars

$60

Roll your dice and race to get the most stars (and sabotage your opponents) on 5 game boards. Each board is filled with treats and tricks—plant flowers (and Piranha Plants) on the delectable Peach’s Birthday Cake board or go intergalactic on the Space Land board. With minigames, star-stealing ghosts, and the troublemaking Bowser, you can turn the tables on your friends and family in an instant. Want to play with far-off friends and family? All modes can be played online!

  • Genre: Party, Multiplayer
  • Multi-tags: Family, Puzzle
  • Party on 5 classic boards from the Nintendo 64 Mario Party games.

  • Play 100 minigames from throughout the Mario Party series.

  • All game modes can be played online

  • Matches with friends in board game mode are saved after each turn both locally and online*—pick up where you left off

  • Play using a single Joy-Con controller, two Joy-Con with the Joy-Con grip accessory, Nintendo Switch™ Pro Controller, or Nintendo Switch Lite system

With a great collection of classic mini-games and a surprising level of challenge, even for experienced players, there’s plenty to love about Mario Party Superstars. While it can be unfair at times, the randomness of the game keeps emotions high, spicing it up for everyone and making each new game fresh. - Gamesbeat
